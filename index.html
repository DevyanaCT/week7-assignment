Rara Lake

Nepal is renowned for its breathtaking lakes, each offering stunning natural beauty and unique experiences. Rara Lake, the largest, captivates with its deep blue waters surrounded by pristine forests. Located in the remote Rara National Park, it is known for its stunning scenery. Its tranquil atmosphere and pristine beauty make Rara Lake a hidden gem in the heart of the Himalayas.

Quick Facts

The lake, spanning 10.8 square kilometers, is a paradise for nature lovers and trekkers, offering breathtaking views and rich biodiversity. The ideal time to visit is from March through May, when the weather is mild and the rhododendrons bloom, filling the mountain trails with color.
